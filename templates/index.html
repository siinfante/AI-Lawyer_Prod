What is AI Lawyer?

AI Lawyer is a portal to showcase AI solutions developed as part of Knowledge Transfer Partnership (KTP) which is an Innovate UK funded project. It aims to transform specialist legal knowledge and expertise in the form of a Decision Support System (DSS) using Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning and Knowledge graphs. These solutions are made available to all the Immigration Law solicitor firms free of cost. Giving access to these solutions for free helps the project to gather more feedback from the solicitor community.

For more information, please refer to an article on the project.
